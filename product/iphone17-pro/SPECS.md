# iPhone 17 Pro: Technical Specifications

## Colors and Finish
- **Colors:** Silver, Cosmic Orange, Deep Blue
- **Finish:** Unibody aluminum structure, Ceramic Shield 2 front, Ceramic Shield back

---

## Capacity
- **Options:** 256GB, 512GB, 1TB

---

## Dimensions and Weight
- **Width:** 71.9 mm
- **Height:** 150.0 mm
- **Thickness:** 8.75 mm
- **Weight:** 204 grams

---

## Display
- **Type:** Super Retina XDR OLED
- **Size:** 6.3 inches
- **Resolution:** 2622 x 1206 pixels at 460 ppi
- **Features:**
  - Dynamic Island
  - Always-On display
  - ProMotion technology with adaptive refresh rates up to 120Hz
  - HDR
  - True Tone
  - Wide color (P3)
  - Contrast Ratio: 2,000,000:1
  - Max brightness of 1000 nits (typical), 1600 nits peak (HDR), 3000 nits peak (outdoor)

---

## Splash, Water, and Dust Resistance
- **Rating:** IP68 (maximum depth of 6 meters up to 30 minutes) under IEC standard 60529

---

## Processor
- **Chip:** A19 Pro
- **CPU:** 6-core (2 performance and 4 efficiency)
- **GPU:** 6-core with neural accelerators
- **Neural Engine:** 16-core
- **Features:** Hardware-accelerated ray tracing

---

## Camera
### Pro Camera System (Fusion Pro)
- **Main:** 48MP, 24 mm, ƒ/1.78 aperture, second-generation sensor-shift optical image stabilization, 100% Focus Pixels
- **Ultra Wide:** 48MP, 13 mm, ƒ/2.2 aperture, 120° field of view, 100% Focus Pixels, 48MP Macro
- **4x Telephoto:** 48MP, 100 mm, ƒ/2.8 aperture, 3D sensor-shift optical image stabilization, tetraprism design, 100% Focus Pixels
- **Zoom:** 2x optical quality (12MP, 48mm), 8x optical quality (12MP, 200mm). 8x optical zoom in, 2x optical zoom out; 16x optical zoom range. Digital zoom up to 40x.

### Video Recording
- **Formats:** 4K at 24/25/30/60/120 fps in Dolby Vision HDR, ProRes RAW up to 4K at 120 fps (external recording), Academy Color Encoding System (ACES), Apple Log 2, Genlock compatible, Spatial video recording.

### Front Camera (Center Stage)
- **Resolution:** 18MP
- **Aperture:** ƒ/1.9
- **Features:** Autofocus with Focus Pixels

---

## Battery and Power
- **Video playback:** Up to 31 hours
- **Video streaming:** Up to 28 hours
- **Charging:** Up to 50% in 20 minutes with a 40W adapter or 30 minutes with a 30W adapter and MagSafe. Compatible with MagSafe wireless charging up to 25W and Qi2 up to 25W.

---

## Security
- **Authentication:** Face ID
- **Emergency Features:** Emergency SOS, Crash Detection

---

## Connectivity
- **Cellular:** 5G (sub-6 GHz) with 4x4 MIMO
- **Wi-Fi:** Wi-Fi 7 (802.11be) with 2x2 MIMO
- **Bluetooth:** Version 6.0
- **Other:** Thread, 2nd generation Ultra Wideband chip, NFC

---

## Port and SIM
- **Port:** USB-C with support for DisplayPort and USB 3 (up to 10 Gb/s)
- **SIM:** Dual eSIM

---

## Operating System
- **OS:** iOS 26
- **Features:** Apple Intelligence
